Welcome! This is a strategy / wiki site for Asuka Kenzan (Dreamcast / PC)
to accompany the English patch & tools.

It's a work-in-progress, so some pages don't have content yet, and others may change in time.
Feel free to follow the project thread over at Romhacking.net.

A significant portion of the information found on this site is hand-translated from the Japanese wiki,
along with the addition of my own personal notes.
