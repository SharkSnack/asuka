Misses somewhat often.

Can pierce through multiple targets.

裏当て身		 3 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Swap.
Swap places with a target in front of you while dealing damage to them.
Piercing Sword
通背剣		 4 - - [Derivative 2] Learned from Swap Strike.
Perform a traveling attack that pierces through a line of enemies.
Demon Lunge
鬼月		 2 500 Lunge forward and deal damage to an enemy up to 4 tiles away.
Tackle
体当		 5 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Demon Lunge.
Move forward 10 tiles. If an enemy is in the way,
grab them and move another 10 tiles, dealing damage to them.
Spirited Throw
気合投げ		 3 600 Throw an enemy in front of you onto a trap or at another enemy.
Circle Throw
八方投げ		 6 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Spirited Throw.
Deal knockback to all enemies in a 1 tile radius.
Parry
受け返し		 4 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Evasion.
Reflect all enemy regular attacks during the turn this is used.
Power Bow
強弓		 1 400 200 Shoot an arrow with x2 power.
Cannot select arrows on the ground or inside pots.
Soft Pitch
軟投		 3 150 Throw an item softly so that it lands on the ground beneath the target.
Cannot select items on the ground or inside pots.
Strong Toss
強肩		 1 200 Throw an item 20 tiles forward.
Cannot select items on the ground or inside pots.
Piercing Toss
遠投		 3 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Strong Toss.
Throw an item so that it pierces through objects and walls.
Cannot select items on the ground or inside pots.
Homing Toss
暇投げ		 4 600 Throw an item at a nearby enemy.
Cannot select items on the ground or inside pots.
Gitan Toss
銭投げ		 6 200 Throw 200 Gitan, which deals 20 damage if it connects.
Coin Toss
小判投げ		 8 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Gitan Toss.
Throw 500 Gitan, which deals 50 damage if it connects.
Large Coin Toss
大判投げ		 10 - - [Derivative 2] Learned from Coin Toss.
Throw 1000 Gitan, which deals 100 damage if it connects.
Blind
目潰		 8 250 Inflict blindness to all enemies in the room for 20 turns.
Cat Clap
猫だまし		 2 300 Paralyze an enemy in front of you for 2 turns.
Tiger Clap
虎だまし		 7 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Cat Clap.
Paralyze all enemies in a 1 tile radius.
Silk Tree Palm
合歓掌		 5 350 Puts the enemy in front of you to sleep for 5 turns.
Slumber Scent
春眠香		 10 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Silk Tree Palm.
Puts all enemies in the room to sleep for 15 turns.
Enemies will have increased movement speed when they wake up.
Imprecation
呪詛		 2 250 Places a deadly curse on an enemy in front of you
that makes them collapse after 15 turns.
Binding Curse
呪縛		 5 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Imprecation.
Places a deadly curse on all enemies within a 1 tile radius
that makes them collapse after 10 turns.
Roaring Thunder
轟天雷		 5 750 [Derivative 1] Learned from Lightning Sword.
Lightning strikes and deals damage to all enemies in the room.
Great Collapse
大崩落		 6 850 Rocks fall and deal damage to all enemies in the room.
Pickpocket
懐盗		 7 450 [Derivative 1] Learned from Walrus Guard.
Steals an item from an enemy in front of you.
Sumo Stamp
四股		 ※10 3500 Makes all enemies in the room trip and drop an item.
Also reveals all hidden traps in the room.
Ignite
発火		 3 400 Sets an item in front of you on fire,
creating a fire pillar that deals 20 damage and lasts for 10 turns.
Will-o-Wisp
鬼火		 6 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Ignite.
Creates a fire pillar in front of you.
Flame God
火神		 8 - - [Derivative 2] Learned from Will-o-Wisp.
Throws a flame that travels 5 tiles forward,
and creates fire pillars 5 tiles to the left and right of a target it hits.
Flood
水浸		 5 700 Creates water puddles on the current floor.
Can be used multiple times to increase the amount of water on the floor.
Flee
高飛び		 5 500 Warps you to a different room on the current floor.
Water Spider
水蜘蛛		 5 900 Lets you walk on water or lava for 30 turns.
Disarm Trap
罠外し		 3 400 Destroys a trap in front of you.
Rock Break
岩石割り		 1 350 Break a wall tile in front of you.
Rock Crush
岩石崩し		 5 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Rock Break.
Creates a tunnel if there's a wall in front of you.
Price Cut
値切		 ※10 2500 Cuts the purchase price in half if you decline once.
Deep Breathing
深呼吸		 5 250 Restores HP roughly equal to 1/2 of your max HP.
Super Metabolism
超代謝		 9 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Deep Breathing.
Restores all HP.
Detox
毒抜き		 5 450 Restores lowered strength to max.
Reversion
復帰		 4 500 Cures negative status conditions. (Confusion, slow, blind, HP drain).
Break Curse
解呪		 10 4 Removes all curses from items in your inventory.
Can also remove curses placed by Jagan Mask family monsters.
Air Meal
霞飯		 6 1500 750 Restores fullness by 30%.
Binge Eating
一気食い		 3 800 Eat multiple grass or food items at once.
Keen Eye
活目		 10 450 Lets you see hidden traps and invisible enemies on the current floor.
Identify
識別		 ※10 1000 500 Identifies 1 item.
Wild Dance
乱舞		 4 400 Changes your stats to x2 attack and 0 defense for 10 turns.
Quick Feet
瞬足		 9 450 Doubles movement speed for 5 turns.
Walrus Guard
トド守り		 6 600 Protects items and Gitan from theft for 100 turns.
Insomnia
不眠		 6 700 Protects against sleep on the current floor.
Clear Mind
心頭滅却		 6 750 Protects against fire damage on the current floor.
Nullify Bombs
爆弾無効		 2 750 Protects against explosion damage on the current floor.
Nullify Lightning
雷無効		 2 750 Protects against electric damage on the current floor.
Dodge
身かわし		 6 1000 Dodge all projectiles on the current floor.
Cannot dodge objects falling from above.
Evasion
見切り		 3 600 Increases your chance of evading enemy attacks for 10 turns.
Rebound
跳返		 8 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Dodge.
Reflects all projectiles for 10 turns.
Cannot reflect objects falling from above.
Barrier
結界		 10 2000 Places a barrier on the ground where you're standing,
preventing adjacent enemies from attacking for 10 turns.
The barrier vanishes if you move.
Invincibility
無敵		 10 - - [Derivative 1] Learned from Barrier.
Makes you invincible to damage for 10 turns.
Cannot prevent status conditions.
Death's Gate
死門		 ※10 10000 Shoots light that seals a monster's existence for the current adventure.
The light has infinite range, but cannot pierce through enemies.
(Cannot seal Managers or Gold Mamels).

※ Must have full spirit meter to use.
