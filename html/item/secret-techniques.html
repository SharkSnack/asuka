Performs 2 regular attacks.

Accurate Sword (必中剣)

Deals an attack that is guaranteed to connect, similar to attacking with a Homing Blade.
This technique can also hit enemies inside walls, through corners, and underwater.

One of the most used techniques in the Trial of Kron dungeon.
It only costs 1 spirit, so use it in these situations:

・Both you and the enemy will collapse in 1 hit.
・An enemy with a troublesome ability is adjacent to you.
・Attack through corners to get an extra hit, similar to Razor Wind.
・Schubell farming, or blocking a room entrance and clearing out enemies on the sides.
・Finishing off an Astral Devil if Parry didn't deal enough damage to defeat it.

Locations:

  • Soaring Bird's Challenge (Floor, Shop)

Lightning Sword (雷神剣)

Performs a lightning elemental attack.
The electricity will travel through and deal damage to all adjacent characters,
including Asuka and NPCs, and will also travel through water puddles.

It's recommended to use Nullify Lightning before use so you don't take damage.
Avoid using it in shops, especially on floors where Ether Devil family monsters appear.

Effective for clearing out Monster Houses using technique chains such as
Nullify Lightning (2) → Wild Dance (4) → Lightning Sword (3)

Tackle (体当)

Move forward 10 tiles.
If an enemy is in the way, grab them and move another 10 tiles, and perform an attack.

Similar to Demon Lunge, this is often used to escape tricky situations.

Parry (受け返し)

Nullifies and reflects damage from attacks back at enemies during the current turn.
Only attacks that connect will be reflected, so missed attacks don't count.

Asuka normally cannot change the direction she's facing after ending her turn,
but she will turn to face the enemy after being attacked while using this technique.
It's possible to equip a Frontal Shield and face away from the enemy before
ending your turn to reflect a large amount of damage.

Particularly effective against frenzied monsters, monster with high attack, or Astral Devil.
(Asuka will turn to face Astral Devil, so use Accurate Sword to finish it off)

Soft Pitch (軟投)

Throw an item that travels 10 tiles and never hits the target.

Pointless to use on its own, but it can be used to trigger traps underneath characters.
Collect items (Trip Stone), riceballs (Spoil Trap), lower stats (Poison and Rust),
seal them (Strip Trap), make them unable to act (Curse and Slow), etc.

It costs 3 spirit like Spirited Throw, so choose what works best for the current situation.
Note that riceballs thrown at Nigiri Trainee family monsters will still one-shot them.

Windows version exclusive.
Throw an item at the closest enemy within 10 tiles.
Asuka will throw the entire stack if a stacked item is selected (ex: 10 Wooden Arrows).

Gitan Toss (銭投げ)

Throw 200 Gitan that travels 10 tiles forward.
The amount is subtracted from your current Gitan total,
and the technique cannot be used if you have less than 200 Gitan.

Deals 20 damage if the Gitan hits the target. Spirit Bullet is a far better option
since it's guaranteed to hit, cannot be reflected, and is half the cost of Gitan Toss.

Ignite (発火)

Burns the item on the ground in front of you, creating a pillar of fire that lasts 10 turns.
No effect if the item in front of you is underwater or in a puddle of water.
Cannot be used if an item is not in front of you.

The pillar of fire behaves the same as those from Fire Staff, Blaze Scroll, and Burn Scroll.

Characters standing in the fire receive 20 damage per turn.
Thrown items burn up, making the fire stronger and extending its duration by 10 turns.
Those standing in the fire when an item burns up will receive an additional 20 damage.
The duration extends by 10 turns when a character collapses and burns up as well.

This technique is primarily used in these situations:

・Turn Spoiled Riceballs into Roasted Riceballs.
・Intentionally lower Asuka's HP to speed up spirit charge rate.
・Fill spirit meter using fire damage near the stairs before advancing to the next floor.
・Level up Inferno for Inferno 99 hunting. (Clear Mind + Imprecation or Binding Curse)

Air Meal (霞飯)

Restores fullness by 30%.
Cannot be used if fullness is already at max.

Spirit charges at the rate of 1 spirit per 50 turns in Trial of Kron when you have full HP.
Therefore, you need to pass 300 turns in order to charge 6 spirit to use this technique,
but fullness will have decreased by 30% when 300 turns have passed.
So you need to find a way to lower your HP to charge spirit faster to actually gain fullness.

That said, there are other ways to restore fullness by 50% or more using 6 spirit.

1. Search for a Spoil Trap. (Keen Eye can be used to reveal all traps on the current floor)
2. Turn an enemy into a Spoiled Riceball using Spirited Throw (3) or Soft Pitch (3).
　・Bladed Wheel can be used instead of Soft Pitch to conserve spirit.
　・Multiple Spoiled Riceballs can be made to increase efficiency.
3. Use Ignite (3) to burn an unneeded item, and step into the fire to turn the Spoiled Riceballs
　into Roasted Riceballs (restores 50% fullness).
　・Landmines can be used instead of Ignite to conserve spirit / actually gain spirit.
　・Roasted Riceballs can be carried and will never spoil, and also restore 30 HP,
　　so it's far better to use this method to restore fullness when possible.
