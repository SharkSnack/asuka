不動の盾		 不 8 3 20000 10000 Prevents Asuka from moving due to outside factors. (ex: Spring, Tiger Tosser)
Also doubles hunger rate.
Dragon Shield
ドラゴンシールド		 竜 10 3 6000 3000 Reduces fire damage by 50%.
Heavy Shield
重装の盾		 重 12 5 2400 1200 Doubles hunger rate.
Frontal Shield
正面戦士の盾		 正 30 5 8000 4000 Doubles defense against attacks coming from forward-facing 3 directions.
However, reduces defense to 0 against attacks coming from
all other directions while also doubling damage received.
Disposable Shield
使い捨ての盾		 捨 40 3 1600 600 Reduces shield strength by 1 every time you receive damage.
Two-Handed Shields
Halberd Shield
矛の盾		 - 7 5 13000 5000 Raises both attack and defense when equipped.
Grand Counter
グランドカウンター		 - 9 9 15000 7500 Reflects 100% of damage received back at monsters.
Yomino Mikogami
黄泉巫女神ノ盾		 - 50 16 60000 30000 Halves damage received, halves hunger rate, blocks poisons, rustproof,
prevents Asuka from moving due to outside factors, reflects magic,
increases evasion, and reflects 1/2 of damage received back at enemies.
