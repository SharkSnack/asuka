Regular Shields

Wooden Shield (鍛えた木の盾)

Low defense, but comes with an upgrade value ranging from +4 to +8.
However, it can be found with an upgrade value of -1 if it's cursed.

Fantastic as a secondary item in synthesis to strengthen a stronger shield.

Equipping a Wooden Sword raises its base defense by 3, but it's still impractical
compared to other shields since it starts with only 2 base defense and 1 seal slot.

Resonance
Wooden Sword (+3 base attack and defense)

Elegant Shield (みやびやかな盾)

Small gold leaf folding screen used as a shield.
Low defense and doesn't have an ability, but it can be sold for a high price.

It has 7 slots, so it can be used to nullify Archdragon fire (竜 + 弟 seals),
or as a main shield in Solidarity when 祭 is synthesized onto it.

Bronze Shield (青銅甲の盾)

Common shield with somewhat low base defense and average slot count.
It's not strong enough on its own to progress through double digit floors,
so it's best to support it with seals like 扇 and 弟.

It lacks an innate ability, so if it's the only shield you come across early game,
synthesize 消 and 弟 and transfer to a stronger shield later on.

Iron Shield (鉄甲の盾)

Well-known shield made of iron. Solid base defense and slot count,
and is a strong option among shields that can be commonly found in dungeons.
Compared to Beast Shield, it has 3 less base defense but 1 more slot,
so it's a trade-off between defense and syntheis slots.

Consider transferring to this shield if you come across an Evasive Shield
or Echo Shield while progressing with a Beast Shield.

Beast Shield (獣王の盾)

High base defense, but 1 less slot count than Iron Shield.
Same base defense as Heavy Shield, but 1 less slot count as well.

Equipment resonance with type effective weapons such as Ghost Sickle
and Drain Buster, dealing an additional 30 damage to those monster types.

It's easy to have Nigiri Morphs make riceballs early game with such high defense,
and the resonance applies to 5 weapons, giving it an edge over other shields.

Fuuma Shield (風魔の盾)

Series staple high defense shield that comes with 6 synthesis slots.
Ideal main shield among those that can be regularly found in dungeons,
but it's extremely rare like Manji-Kabura, so don't expect to obtain it.

Becomes Helix Shield when 金, 山, 識, 眠, う, 見, 飯, 身 seals are synthesized.
The clear reward Fuuma Shield of Koga's Hidden Hole has 8 slots (PC ver.),
and the clear reward Fuuma Shield of Reverse White Snake Isle
comes with the 返 seal, which allows you to reflect projectiles.

Hoshino Mikoshi (星神輿ノ盾)

If you lose this shield, it can be found either on the same floor of the dungeon
that you collapsed in, or in the left room on the deepest floor of Firework Cannon.
This is the case even when the shield is lost due to a Kengo family monster
disarming you, or if the shield is inserted into a Presto Pot.
Upgrade value and seals are saved even if the shield is lost. Cannot be sold.

Very high base defense, and comes with 10 synthesis slots.
The greatest advantage of this shield is the fact that it can't be permanently lost.
It therefore makes for a solid main shield for dungeons that allow carry-in items,
especially if making a Helix Shield sounds like too much of a hassle.

However, you'll often have valuable unbreakable bracelets or expanded Storage Pots
when you explore dungeons at end game, so it's a given that you won't collapse.
It's also possible to prevent equipment loss by synthesizing the 帰 seal (Retreat Scroll),
so it might not see much use overall.

It's not mentioned in the in-game description, but this shield cannot be used as a
secondary item in synthesis. There's a glitch that spawns a second Hoshino Mikoshi
if you collapse in a dungeon after the shield's seals change within a dungeon.

How to Obtain
Found in the room to the left on the deepest floor of Firework Cannon.
If you lose the shield, it can be found in the same room again.

Helix Shield (ラセン風魔の盾)

Strongest shield in the game, with no others coming close to its 99 base defense.
(Asuka's defense reaches 295 at +99 upgrade value)

Only obtainable through a special synthesis recipe.
It can be a hassle to make it, but it's definitely worth the effort.
Synthesize Fuuma Stones to increase its slot count up to a max of 16 slots.

Resonance
Fiery Fuuma (+5 base attack and defense)
How to Obtain
Synthesize 金, 山, 識, 眠, う, 見, 飯, 身 onto a Fuuma Shield. (Seal order doesn't matter)
Fuuma Shield has a default slot count of 6 slots, so either synthesize 2 Fuuma Stones,
or use an 8 slot Fuuma Shield obtained from clearing Koga's Hidden Hole. (PC version)

Special Ability Shields

Nirvana Shield (サトリの盾)

Stops hunger, but also lowers max fullness to 1%.
Cannot increase max fullness above 1% while this shield is equipped,
and max fullness remains at 1% if the shield is unequipped.

Laughing Polygon family monsters can still lower your fullness,
and wearing Monster Boxes still depletes fullness since equipment is ignored,
so it's best to synthesize a Bowl Shield or keep some grass items on hand.

If max fullness decreases to 0% due to a Singing Polygon,
simply unequip the shield and equip it again to increase it back to 1%.
However, fullness will be at 0%, so you'll want to eat something.

How to Obtain
Synthesize 3 重 seals onto a Heavy Shield. (4 Heavy Shields total)

Leather Shield (皮の盾)

Shield that halves hunger rate and never rusts.
Since it's an innate ability, Lv4 Slimes cannot lower this shield's upgrade value.
(But they can still erase synthesis seals)
The 皮 seal only slows hunger, and does not include the rustproof effect.

Halving hunger rate greatly lowers the risk of collapsing from starvation.
It doubles the number of turns you have to hunt monsters for items and experience,
check each room for items, and makes it easier to step in place to restore HP.

This shield essentially has 7 slots which is great, but base defense is extremely
important in Asuka, so it doesn't see much use as a main shield.
You'll feel the lack of defense before you even reach double digit floors in
dungeons like Reverse White Snake Isle, so aim to use it as a secondary shield,
or as a synthesis seal for a stronger shield.
