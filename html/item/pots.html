Asuka Kenzan Wiki / Pots

Pot Summary

Synthesis Pot can be found in sizes 2~4, and all other pots can be found in sizes 3~6.

※ English names might change as the translation project progresses.

Name Buy +1 Sell +1 Info
Heal Pot
回復の壺		 1000 50 500 25 Insert an item to restore all of your HP.
(The inserted item will be lost)
Water Pot
水がめ		 Can scoop water, which can be used in a variety of ways.
Degrade Pot
弱化の壺		 Decreases inserted equipment upgrade value or staff uses by 3.
Storage Pot
保存の壺		 1200 60 600 30 Can take items out after being inserted.
Sale Pot
換金の壺		 Inserted items are turned into Gitan equal to their selling price.
Identify Pot
識別の壺		 1500 75 750 37.5 Inserted items are identified.
Presto Pot
変化の壺		 Inserted items transform into different items.
Hide Pot
やりすごしの壺		 2000 100 1000 50 Lets you hide inside of the pot for 10 turns.
(Can be used to evade Jagan Mask's deadly curse countdown)
Void Pot
底抜けの壺		 Inserted items vanish, and pitfalls appear if shattered.
Sticky Pot
手封じの壺		 Your hand gets stuck inside the pot if you try to insert items.
(Punch walls or monsters to free your hand)
Mail Pot
倉庫の壺		 3000 150 1500 75 Inserted items are sent to the storage facility.
Synthesis Pot
合成の壺		 4000 200 2000 100 Insert items to fuse them together. Can only synthesize
weapons with weapons, shields with shields, or staves with staves.
Blessing Pot
祝福の壺		 5000 250 Inserted items will become blessed when the pot shatters.
Upgrade Pot
強化の壺		 15000 750 7500 375 Increases inserted equipment upgrade value or staff uses by 1.
