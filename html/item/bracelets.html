Asuka Kenzan Wiki / Bracelets

Bracelet Summary

※ English names might change as the translation project progresses.

Name Buy Sell Info
Calm Bracelet
混乱よけの腕輪		 2000 1000 Prevents confusion.
Rustproof Bracelet
サビよけの腕輪		 Prevents equipment from rusting.
Can also block level 4 Slime's seal erasing special attack.
Sleepless Bracelet
ねむらずの腕輪		 Prevents falling asleep.
Holy Bracelet
呪いよけの腕輪		 Protects Asuka and her items from curses.
This bracelet cannot be cursed.
Bind Bracelet
必着の腕輪		 Prevents equipment from being disarmed.
Warp Bracelet
たかとびの腕輪		 3000 1500 Sometimes causes Asuka to warp when she receives damage.
Strength Bracelet
ちからの腕輪		 Increases strength by 3 points.
Thief Bracelet
とうぞくの腕輪		 Sleeping monsters will never wake up when you walk next to them.
They also don't wake up when you step into a Monster House.
Wish Bracelet
ねがいの腕輪		 Something good might happen when it shatters.
Pierce Bracelet
遠投の腕輪		 4000 2000 Thrown items pierce through objects and walls.
Bullseye Bracelet
百発百中の腕輪		 Thrown items always hit their target.
Bend Bracelet
まがりの腕輪		 Thrown items curve when they hit a wall.
Heal Bracelet
いやしの腕輪		 5000 2500 Boosts natural HP regeneration to x1.5 of the normal amount.
Anti Drain Bracelet
ドレインよけの腕輪		 Protects you from Drain type monster special attacks.
Vision Bracelet
よくみえの腕輪		 Lets you see hidden traps and invisible monsters.
Critical Bracelet
会心の腕輪		 6000 3000 Grants a 1/4 chance to deal a critical hit.
Regret Bracelet
痛恨の腕輪		 Occasionally receive brutal hits that deal x1.5 damage.
Protect Bracelet
まもりの腕輪		 7500 Reduces damage received by 25%.
Substitute Bracelet
身代わりの腕輪		 Revives you if you collapse while it's equipped.
(The bracelet is lost after being revived)
Float Bracelet
通過の腕輪		 10000 5000 Lets you walk on water and lava tiles.
Scout Bracelet
透視の腕輪		 Displays monster and item locations on the map.
Phantasm Bracelet
パコレプキンの腕輪		 Lets you walk inside walls, and also allows you to walk on water and lava tiles.
Receive damage equal to 5% of your max HP while inside walls.
Bargain Bracelet
値切の腕輪		 12000 6000 Decline once to purchase items at half price while shopping.
Parry Bracelet
うけながしの腕輪		 15000 7500 Redirects damage to another monster within a 1 tile radius.
Happy Bracelet
しあわせの腕輪		 Gain 1 experience point per turn while equipped.
No Hunger Bracelet
ハラヘラズの腕輪		 Stops hunger.
Hunger Bracelet
ハラヘリの腕輪		 Doubles hunger rate.
Spirit Bracelet
気力の腕輪		 25000 1000 Lets you to charge spirit and use secret techniques.
Identify Bracelet
識別の腕輪		 12000 Identify items by picking them up.
Trapper Bracelet
ワナ師の腕輪		 10000 Reveals hidden traps, and makes it so that monsters can be trapped.
