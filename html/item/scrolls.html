Asuka Kenzan Wiki / Scrolls

Scroll Summary

※ English names might change as the translation project progresses.

Name Seal Buy Sell Info
Trap Scroll
ワナの巻物		 - 200 100 Generates many traps in the room.
Identify Scroll
識別の巻物		 識 500 250 Identifies the selected item.
Light Scroll
あかりの巻物		 - Reveals the current floor's map layout + enemy and item locations.
Water Scroll
水びたしの巻物		 - Creates water puddles throughout the current floor.
Burn Scroll
火種の巻物		 - Burns all items on the ground inside the current room.
Retreat Scroll
引き上げの巻物		 帰 Return to the starting village with your equipped items.
(Note that all other items and Gitan will be lost)
Purify Scroll
おはらいの巻物		 祓 1000 500 Removes curses from all items, excluding items inside pots.
(This scroll cannot be cursed)
Fear Scroll
ゾワゾワの巻物		 - Inflicts fear to all enemies in a 1 tile radius.
Accuracy Scroll
必中の巻物		 - Regular attacks will never miss on the current floor.
(Asuka, allies, and enemies)
Cheer Scroll
ファイトの巻物		 - Boosts attack and defense by x1.5 for 20 turns.
(Asuka and allies)
Trap Remove Scroll
ワナけしの巻物		 - Removes all traps in the room.
Immobilize Scroll
はりつけの巻物		 - Prevents characters in the room from moving for 30 turns.
(Asuka can still move like normal)
Gravity Scroll
飛ばずの巻物		 - Projectiles will immediately fall to the ground for 30 turns.
(Effect applies to all characters on the current floor)
Escape Scroll
持ち帰りの巻物		 - Return to the starting village with all of your items and Gitan.
Heaven Scroll
天の恵みの巻物		 - 1200 600 Increases weapon upgrade value by 1, and removes a curse if present.
Earth Scroll
地の恵みの巻物		 - Increases shield upgrade value by 1, and removes a curse if present.
Slumber Scroll
バクスイの巻物		 眠 Puts all characters in the room + 1 tile radius to sleep for 15 turns.
(Characters will have increased movement speed when they wake up)
Blaze Scroll
炎上の巻物		 - Sets the surrounding area (24 tiles) on fire for 10 turns.
Confusion Scroll
混乱の巻物		 - 1500 750 Confuses characters in the room for 20 turns.
(Confuses characters in a 1 tile radius if read in a hallway)
Rockfall Scroll
落石の巻物		 - Deals 30~40 damage to all enemies in the room.
(Deals damage to enemies in a 1 tile radius if read in a hallway)
Lightning Scroll
いかずちの巻物		 - Deals 20~35 damage to all enemies in the room.
(Deals damage to enemies in a 1 tile radius if read in a hallway)
Helpful Scroll
困った時の巻物		 - Various positive effects can occur based on your current situation.
Trouble Scroll
困った巻物		 - Some sort of negative effect occurs when read.
Monster House Scroll
モンスターハウスの巻物		 - Turns the current room into a Monster House.
(Warps you to another room beforehand if read inside a shop or hallway)
Evaporate Scroll
水がれの巻物		 - 2000 1000 All water on the current floor evaporates.
Wall Collapse Scroll
大部屋の巻物		 - Turns the current floor into one giant room.
Sturdy Pot Scroll
壺われずの巻物		 - The selected pot can no longer shatter during the current adventure.
Pot Expand Scroll
壺増大の巻物		 - Increases the size of the selected pot by 1.
Transfer Scroll
転写の巻物		 - Can copy the effect of any other scroll you have on hand.
Suction Scroll
すいだしの巻物		 - 3000 1500 Suctions out all contents from a pot.
Plating Scroll
メッキの巻物		 - 4000 2000 Adds the 金 (rustproof) seal to the selected weapon or shield.
Can be used to repair cracked bracelets as well.
Blank Scroll
白紙の巻物		 - 5000 2500 Write down any scroll you've read before, and then use this as that scroll.
Sanctuary Scroll
聖域の巻物		 - 7500 Place this scroll on the ground to create a safe zone where monsters
next to the scroll cannot attack anyone standing on top of the scroll.
(Projectiles and staff magic can still hit you, so you're not invincible)
Extinction Scroll
ねだやしの巻物		 - 25000 400 Throw at a monster to make that monster vanish from the current adventure.
(Same name only, so you can't seal an entire monster family away)
Technique Scroll
奥技の巻物		 - 1500 750 Relearn a secret technique that you've learned in the past.
